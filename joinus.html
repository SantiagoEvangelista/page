What we work on

Signal Processing

Developing novel algorithms that extract positioning data from non-navigation RF signals in real-time.

Sensor Fusion

Combining opportunistic measurements with inertial data for continuous, drift-free navigation.

Custom Hardware

Designing RF front-ends and embedded platforms optimized for SWaP-constrained deployment.

Contested Environments

Ensuring our systems perform when existing infrastructure is degraded, denied, or deceptive.
