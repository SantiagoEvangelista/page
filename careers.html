Careers

We're building navigation systems that work where GPS does not. Select a role to learn more.

Design and implement signal acquisition and processing chains for opportunistic navigation. You will work across the full RF pipeline, from antenna interface to position solution.

Responsibilities
  • Develop signal processing algorithms for non-GNSS signal sources
  • Design and characterize RF front-end architectures
  • Build multi-source fusion pipelines for resilient positioning
  • Prototype and validate in real-world degraded environments
Background
  • Strong foundation in DSP, estimation theory, or statistical signal processing
  • Experience with SDR platforms and RF measurement
  • Proficiency in C/C++ or MATLAB for algorithm development
  • Familiarity with navigation concepts (PNT, INS, sensor fusion)

Build the real-time processing core of our navigation hardware. You will design FPGA architectures and embedded firmware that run signal processing algorithms under strict latency and power constraints.

Responsibilities
  • Architect and implement FPGA designs for real-time signal processing
  • Develop embedded firmware on ARM/RISC-V platforms
  • Optimize processing pipelines for latency, throughput, and power
  • Design hardware/software interfaces and bring-up new boards
Background
  • Experience with FPGA development (VHDL or Verilog) on Xilinx or Intel platforms
  • Embedded C/C++ on bare-metal or RTOS environments
  • Understanding of digital signal processing at the hardware level
  • Comfortable with lab tools: oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, spectrum analyzers

Own the path from prototype to delivered hardware. You will manage component sourcing, contract manufacturing, and quality assurance for low-volume, high-reliability navigation systems.

Responsibilities
  • Source and qualify electronic components and RF parts
  • Manage relationships with contract manufacturers
  • Define and enforce quality and testing procedures
  • Build inventory and logistics processes for low-volume production
Background
  • Experience in hardware production, procurement, or supply chain for electronics
  • Familiarity with PCB/PCBA manufacturing processes
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Experience with defense or aerospace supply chains is a plus
